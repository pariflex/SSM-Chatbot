Nursing Program

M.Sc.Nursing

M.Sc. Nursing, short for Master of Science in Nursing is a postgraduate level programme. The course aims at catering to the field of Nursing and Applied Sciences.

Eligibility: Relevant Bachelor’s Degree

Selection Process: Direct Admissions or Entrance Examinations

Duration: 2 Year

Fee Detail

B.Sc.Nursing

B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science) To pursue B. Sc. nursing the candidates should have minimum eligibility criteria, having completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as mandatory subjects. 

Eligibility : 10+2 (45% – 50% in Qualifying Exam)

Selection Process: Entrance Examinations

Duration: 4 Year

Fee Detail

P.B.B.Sc.Nursing

B.Sc. Nursing (Post Basic) is a two-year duration undergraduate course in nursing. … In post basic BSc Nursing (also referred to as PBBSc), candidates are provided a holistic mixture of classroom lectures and hands-on training.

Eligibility : 10+2 and GNM

Selection Process: Direct Admissions or Entrance Examinations

Duration: 2 Year

Fee Detail

G.N.M.

General Nursing and Midwifery or GNM is a three-year and six-month diploma course designed for aspirants who want to pursue a career in clinical nursing.

Eligibility : 10+2with English (Science, Arts or Commerce streams)

Selection Process: Entrance Examinations

Duration:4 Year

Education Program

M.Ed.

M.Ed. is the short form for Master of Education, which is typically dealing with the pedagogical aspects of teaching. In a few cases, The M.Ed. course covers areas like curriculum and instruction, counseling, school psychology, and administration.

Eligibility : Minimum Percentage of 50% to 60% in the relevant graduation course ( Like B.Ed)

Selection Process: Merit Based/Entrance Exam based

Duration: 2 Year

Fee Detail

B.Ed.

Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) is an undergraduate course offered for those interested in pursuing a career in teaching. degree is mandatory for teaching at the secondary (classes 6 to 10) and higher secondary (10+2 or classes 11 and 12).

Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream with 50% marks

Selection Process: Merit Based/Entrance Exam based

Duration: 2 Year

Fee Detail

D.El.Ed.

DElEd, full form Diploma in Elementary Education, is a 2-years diploma course focused on primary teachers training. DElEd provides training to the would-be teachers to teach students in the primary/upper primary school, aged between 6-15 years/class I to VIII.

Eligibility: 10+2 in any stream with 50% marks

Selection Process: Merit Based

Duration: 2 Year
